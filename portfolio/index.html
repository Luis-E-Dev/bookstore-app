The Transition Story

My career began in the Salesforce ecosystem, where I spent years building custom solutions for enterprise clients. I became proficient in Apex, Lightning Web Components, and navigating the intricacies of the Salesforce platform.

But I wanted more. The constraints of a proprietary platform sparked my curiosity about broader software engineering principles. I began exploring modern web frameworks, diving into JavaScript, Python, and cloud infrastructure. What started as evening learning sessions evolved into a complete career pivot.

Today, I apply the problem-solving skills honed in Salesforce development to build full-stack applications. My unique background gives me perspective on both enterprise systems and modern software architecture, allowing me to bridge gaps and deliver solutions that work in the real world.
